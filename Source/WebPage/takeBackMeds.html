A brief overview!

Prescription Drug Take Back programs are designed to remove unused, unneeded, or expired medications from the home and dispose of them safely in order to prevent poisonings, prevent prescription drug abuse, and protect the environment. Drug take back events collect medications from individuals and households and excludes pharmacies, long-term care facilities, or other healthcare providers..
